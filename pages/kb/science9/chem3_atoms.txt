[R1] Atoms and Molecules — Key Points
• Laws: Conservation of mass; Constant proportions (fixed ratio in compounds).
• Atomic mass (u); Molecular mass = sum of atomic masses.
• Mole: 1 mol = 6.022×10^23 particles. n (mol) = mass (g) / molar mass (g/mol).
• Valency method to write formulae; practice with polyatomic ions (SO4^2-, NO3^-, CO3^2-).
