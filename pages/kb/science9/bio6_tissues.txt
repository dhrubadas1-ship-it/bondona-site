[R1] Tissues — Key Points
• Plant: Meristematic (apical, intercalary, lateral). Permanent (simple: parenchyma, collenchyma, sclerenchyma; complex: xylem, phloem).
• Animal: Epithelial (simple/stratified), Connective (areolar, adipose, bone, cartilage, blood), Muscular (skeletal, smooth, cardiac), Nervous (neurons, neuroglia).
